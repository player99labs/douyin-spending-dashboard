# Douyin Spending Dashboard

A drag-and-drop dashboard to see where your Douyin 💎 actually goes.

I watch livestreams on Douyin and tip streamers. I wonder who I tipped the most so I built one.

**Live:** https://player99labs.github.io/douyin-spending-dashboard/

- Download CSV of your spending on Douyin - 我的钱包-钻石明细-...-下载明细
- Drop in your CSV — see totals, monthly trends, and top streamers in seconds
- Per-month pie chart of streamer distribution
- Nickname streamers (use the same nickname on multiple accounts to merge them into one)
- Searchable transaction log with both raw account ID and your nickname
- All data stays in your browser — nothing uploaded, ever
- Single HTML file, no install, Chinese UI
